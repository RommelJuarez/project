Site Plan: Nature Cosmetics

Site Name

Name: Nature Cosmetics

This name was chosen because it reflects the brand's focus on natural, eco-friendly cosmetic products. The word "Nature" connects with the environmentally-conscious audience, while "Cosmetics" clearly defines the product category. This name aims to attract consumers looking for sustainable and organic beauty solutions.

Optional Domain Availability: nature-cosmetics.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Nature Cosmetics website is to provide a platform for customers to learn about our range of eco-friendly, natural beauty products. It will also offer tips on skincare routines, detailed information about the ingredients we use, and an e-commerce store for purchasing products online.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What are the best organic ingredients for sensitive skin?
  • Scenario 2: How can I purchase Nature Cosmetics products online?

Color Scheme

Main Colors:

  • Green (#6EA41C): Used for headings and buttons to represent nature and freshness.
  • Light Grey (#c9cbcb): Used for the background and accents to give a clean, natural look.

Typography

Font Family: "Poppins", sans-serif

Heading Font: Poppins

Paragraph Font: Poppins, sans-serif
